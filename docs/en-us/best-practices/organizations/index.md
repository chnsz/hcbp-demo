# Introduction

## What is Organizations

Organizations is a multi-account management service provided by Huawei Cloud, supporting enterprises to centrally manage multiple Huawei Cloud accounts through organizational structure, achieving centralized resource management and unified permission control. Organizations service supports creating organizations, organizational units, and accounts, building multi-account architectures, achieving resource isolation, cost allocation, and fine-grained permission management.

Organizations service provides complete organizational structure management functions, supporting policy inheritance, account migration, tag management, and other functions, with enterprise-level multi-account management capabilities, simplifying resource management and operation work in multi-account environments. Through Organizations service, enterprises can achieve unified management in multi-account architectures, improving resource management efficiency and security.
